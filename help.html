What is VaultsPay?

VaultsPay is a payment solutions provider. Our technology allows people to buy online, pay bills or family, and more. Businesses can also use our services to seamlessly provide their own customers with secure payment options.

What kinds of payments can I accept with VaultsPay?

VaultsPay enables customers to use, and merchants to accept, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and most credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express. VaultsPay also offers secure and innovative contactless payment options like QR Code.
