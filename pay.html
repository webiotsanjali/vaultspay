How QR Code works?

  • 1

    Create your account

    Create and log-in to your account, adding the card or bank details you want to use

  • 2

    Select payment option

    At the participating store or venue checkout, select 'Pay with QR Code' within the VaultsPay app

  • 3

    Scan the QR code

    Scan the merchant's QR Code, add the amount and confirm. You can add any promotion codes before confirming too!

  • 4

    Receive confirmation

    You and the merchant will receive a notification once the transaction has successfully completed.
